About Us

Welcome to Padharo.Sa

From the aromatic spices of North India to the coastal delights of the South and the vibrant cuisine of the West, our Thalis take you on a journey to capture the essence of each region.

Our thali at Padharo.Sa takes traditional thalis to an elegant space. Each thali comes with over 22 items including appetizers, dals, curries, breads, assortments, and sweets. We bring you a diverse assortment of flavors in one delightful experience.
